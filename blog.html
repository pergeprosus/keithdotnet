the old internet
11/22/23

Since childhood, I've been captivated by the internet, a cyber-jungle brimming with diverse and unpredictable content. In its early days, it was a realm of authentic, whimsical discoveries, from videos of peers' shenanigans to bizarre, hidden gems. Nowadays, it's transformed into a landscape dominated by monotony and artificiality, losing its unique social spaces. Rest in peace, old internet.
